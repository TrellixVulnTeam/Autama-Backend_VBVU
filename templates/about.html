About Us

Thanks for checking out Autama.org!


This is a new community project to test new experiences with quirky personalities that we generate using AI in collaboration with humans. We've coined those personalities Autama and we see them as a step up above a lame ol' bot. (even though sometimes they are much dumber than a bot.)


We'd like to invite you to join us if you think this is a fun project. We have a variety of areas where we would like to advance this high-ish fidelity prototype.

These include:

  • Leveling up the interface through the ux process
  • Continuing app development
  • Digging into chat to improve the experience
  • Making Autama personalities richer through additional corpus or modifications


    • Please reach out to myself or Sharice the team lead, if you would like to participate and we'll find a place for you to become a happyslacker.
